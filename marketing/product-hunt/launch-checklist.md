# Product Hunt Launch Checklist

This checklist outlines key steps and best practices for a successful Product Hunt launch.

## Pre-Launch (2-4 Weeks Before)

*   **Product Hunt Profile:** Ensure your personal and product profiles are complete.
*   **Community Engagement:** Actively upvote and comment on other products to build credibility.
*   **Launch Assets:** Prepare all visuals (screenshots, GIFs, videos) and compelling copy (tagline, description).
*   **Landing Page:** Design a dedicated landing page optimized for Product Hunt traffic with a clear CTA.
*   **Identify Supporters:** Create a list of early users, friends, colleagues, and influencers who can genuinely support your launch.
*   **Draft Marketing Materials:** Prepare email templates, social media posts, and messages for various platforms.
*   **Hunter Selection:** Decide if you will launch as a maker or work with a well-known hunter for increased visibility.

## Launch Day

*   **Timing:** Launch at 12:01 AM PT on a Tuesday or Wednesday for maximum visibility throughout the day.
*   **Post Your Product:**
    *   Upload all assets (images, GIFs, videos).
    *   Write a compelling, concise tagline.
    *   Include the authentic Maker's Comment, sharing your story and vision.
*   **Promote & Share (Legitimately):**
    *   Share your Product Hunt link on social media platforms (LinkedIn, Twitter, Reddit), email lists, Slack communities, and relevant forums.
    *   **Crucially, DO NOT directly ask for upvotes.** Instead, ask for feedback, support, or to check out your launch. For example: "We just launched on Product Hunt! Check it out and let us know what you think!"
    *   Engage with your early supporters and community.
*   **Engage Actively:**
    *   Monitor comments and questions on your Product Hunt page throughout the day.
    *   Respond promptly and authentically to all feedback, positive or negative.
    *   Share milestones (e.g., "Top 10 today!") on social media.
*   **Team Coordination:** If you have a team, create a "shift plan" for who will monitor and engage at different times.

## Post-Launch

*   **Thank Supporters:** Express gratitude to everyone who engaged with your launch.
*   **Analyze Results:** Review traffic, sign-ups, and feedback to measure your success against your goals.
*   **Follow Up:** Continue to respond to any lingering comments or questions.
*   **Repurpose Content:** Use your launch assets and insights in future marketing efforts.

## Who to Notify

*   **Your team and colleagues.**
*   **Early users and beta testers.**
*   **Friends and family (who genuinely support your work).**
*   **Influencers or mentors** you've engaged with who might genuinely be interested.
*   **Relevant communities** (Slack, Discord, Reddit, forums) where your product solves a problem, but *always follow community rules* and focus on sharing, not spamming for upvotes.
*   **Your existing email list.**
